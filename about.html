Who We Are

Safety Equipment & Branded Workwear Since 2010

Frontmax is a Nairobi-based supplier of premium safety tools, PPE, and custom-branded overalls. We operate from Lunga Lunga Plaza in the Industrial Area, serving construction, manufacturing, logistics, and healthcare clients across Kenya.

We do not just sell — we brand. Our workshop handles embroidery, screen printing, and reflective strip application on overalls, helmets, and safety vests. Same-day pickup available for stocked items.
